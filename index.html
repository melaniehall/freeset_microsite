Getting Started

Our custom bags offer you:

  • Wide range of easy-to-customize styles
  • 20 jute colors to mix and match
  • 3 colors of 100% organic cotton sheeting
  • 3 colors of 100% organic cotton canvas
  • Screen printing for up to 6 colors per design
  • Environmentally friendly products
  • Ethical manufacturing in line with fair trade princples
  • Opportunity to make a real contribution to the freedom of women!

Get started by choosing one of our designs. Next, select your color scheme, add or subtract features as desired, and send us the artwork you want printed on the bags! If you don't see exactly what you're looking for, feel free to contact us and request a quote.*

**There is a 100 bag minimum applies for all custom orders. All bags must be the same style in the same color with the same print. Please contact Dan Lander at sales@freesetglobal.com for any questions.
